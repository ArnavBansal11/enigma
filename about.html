About Us

We are Enigma, the coveted Quiz Society of Delhi Public School, Noida.

We started out nearly a decade ago, and today, we are one of the most elite quiz clubs in Delhi-NCR with almost all titles under out belt, which include the Columban Open Quiz (three times), which is considered by many as the biggest quiz in North India.

Recently, we have undergone a major overhaul, renaming ourselves from Qriosity to Enigma. The redesign also included a new logo, new badges, sweatshirts, etc. Last week, we hosted Encrypt, our very own intra-school quiz, which saw the participation of a whopping 110 teams, which is far greater than most inter-school events.

We are always open to welcome bright and young minds into the grand legacy of the club. Newcomers can join us here.
